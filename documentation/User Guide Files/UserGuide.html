Hello and welcome to Where to Poop.

How to Access our App

In the Browser:

To open the application in a web browser:

In your web browser, type "https://adeeb10abbas.github.io/WhereToPoop/" without the quotes in the address bar.

How to Use the App

Navigation

To navigate between different areas of the site, scroll up and down the page or click the buttons on the side

Features Section

The Features section displays the Where to Poop map and provides basic information about the app's services

The first thing you will see is the map. The map has icons on it indicating which different types of bathrooms are available. An icon that looks like a man indicates a men's bathroom is present there. An icon that looks like a woman indicates that a women's bathroom is available there. An icon that looks like a baby indicates that baby changing facilities are available. An icon that looks like both the male and female symbols indicates a gender neutral bathroom.

To view a restroom, click an icon on the map. The side panel on the map will open and display the rating and reviews for the bathroom. If the side panel is opened when a bathroom is not selected, it will display a list of nearby bathrooms.

Our Team

Each developer under this section has a link to their LinkedIn page when their name is clicked. This can be used to contact one or more developers if you have questions or an issue. You can also view their Github portfolios or follow them on Twitter if you click the appropriate buttons

Get Started

To add a toilet, click "Add a Toilet" and fill out the required information on the Google form. Your toilet will be vetted for its existence as a public restroom before being added to the map.

To add a review, click the "Add a Review" button and fill out the required information on the Google form.

Other Contact Information

At the bottom of the website is a list of the best contact information to reach our team as a whole and a general comments box.
